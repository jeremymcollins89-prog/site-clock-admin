Payroll email

Where "Submit Hours for Payroll" sends timesheet emails from your employees. Required before employees can submit hours.

Pay schedule

How often your pay periods repeat. This controls what "Submit Hours for Payroll" bundles together.

Pick the first day of any one pay period you already know (e.g. the start of last pay period). Every period is calculated from this date.

Change admin email

This is the email you sign into this app with. Changing it requires your current password.

Shop location

This is the location employees auto clock-in at when they arrive (and auto clock-out from when they leave). Tip: right-click your shop's spot in Google Maps, click the coordinates that pop up to copy them, then paste the first number below as Latitude and the second as Longitude.

Employees still clocked in "In Town" get auto clocked out at this time if they've left the shop. Doesn't apply to "Traveling" shifts.
